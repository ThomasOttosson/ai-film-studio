# CODE_OF_CONDUCT

## Our Pledge

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Welcome feedback.
- Collaborate professionally.
- Respect differing viewpoints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Trolling or intimidation.
- Publishing private information.
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct and may restrict participation when necessary.

## Reporting

Report violations privately to the project maintainers or the security contact listed in SECURITY.md.
