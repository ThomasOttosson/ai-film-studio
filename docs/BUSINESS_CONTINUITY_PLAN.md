# Business Continuity Plan (BCP)

## Purpose

This document describes how AI Film Studio maintains critical business operations during disruptive events.

## Objectives

- Minimize downtime.
- Protect customer data.
- Restore essential services quickly.
- Maintain communication with stakeholders.

## Critical Services

- Authentication
- API services
- Media storage
- Rendering pipeline
- Monitoring and alerting

## Continuity Strategies

- Redundant infrastructure
- Automated backups
- Infrastructure as Code
- Documented recovery procedures
- Cross-trained maintainers

## Incident Response

1. Assess impact.
2. Activate incident response.
3. Restore critical services.
4. Verify service health.
5. Communicate status updates.
6. Perform post-incident review.

## Communications

Maintain communication with:
- Users
- Internal maintainers
- Infrastructure providers
- Security contacts

## Testing

Review and exercise this plan at least annually and after significant infrastructure changes.
