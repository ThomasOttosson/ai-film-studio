# Third-Party Risk Management

## Purpose

This document defines how AI Film Studio evaluates, approves, monitors, and reviews third-party services, libraries, and vendors.

## Objectives

- Reduce supply-chain risk.
- Protect customer and project data.
- Ensure service reliability.
- Support regulatory and contractual obligations.

## Vendor Evaluation

Before adoption, evaluate:

- Security posture
- Service availability
- Data handling practices
- Compliance certifications where applicable
- Financial and operational stability
- Incident response capabilities
- Support quality
- Exit strategy

## Approval

New third-party services should:

1. Have a documented business need.
2. Receive technical review.
3. Receive security review for sensitive integrations.
4. Be approved by the appropriate project owner.

## Ongoing Monitoring

Review providers periodically for:

- Security advisories
- Availability issues
- Contract changes
- Privacy policy changes
- End-of-life announcements

## Offboarding

When a provider is retired:

- Remove credentials.
- Delete unnecessary stored data.
- Revoke access.
- Update documentation.
- Validate successful migration where applicable.

## Review

Review this document annually and after significant supplier changes.
